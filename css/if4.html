Causes of Global Warming

If your house is on fire without any room for ventilation, it'll lead to suffocation. The same scenario occurs in case of the earth - it is trapped in 'greenhouse gases'. The combination of molecules in the atmosphere, which is made up of water vapor, methane, ozone, nitrous oxide, and carbon dioxide, are called greenhouse gases. They trap the heat from the sunrays and prevent the planet below from heating up.
